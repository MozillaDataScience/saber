Data Assets¶

This experiment launched on 2019-12-17, and targeted 0.2 of Firefox Users on version . These users were randomly sampled and assigned one of the 3 experimental conditions: ['control', 'treatment-banner', 'treatment-no-banner'].

We evaluated 9 measures of interactions in this study on a per user basis:

ad_clicks
organic_search_count
search_count
searches_with_ads
separate_search_engine
